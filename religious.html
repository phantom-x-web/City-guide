Temples & Shrines of Vizianagaram

VIZIANAGARAM TEMPLES

A city of deep spiritual heritage, Vizianagaram is home to ancient temples, sacred hilltops, and devotional spaces that have been centres of worship for centuries.

🛕
01

Ramanarayanam

Korukonda Road, Vizianagaram

Represents the life and values of Lord Rama. A calm and peaceful environment that inspires reflection and devotion.

🛕
02

Subrahmanyaswamy Temple

Phool Bagh Colony Road

Dedicated to Lord Subrahmanya. Believed to fulfill prayers for health, prosperity, and success.

🛕
03

Pydithalli Temple

Three Lamps Area

Dedicated to Goddess Pydithalli Ammavaru. A site of immense significance, especially during the Dasara festival.

🛕
04

Dwadasa Jyotirlinga Temple

SVN Nagar

Dedicated to Lord Shiva. A rare and sacred place where all 12 Jyotirlingas are enshrined together under one roof.

🛕
05

Gnyana Saraswati Temple

Ring Road, SVN Nagar

Dedicated to Goddess Saraswati. A serene temple offering a peaceful devotional atmosphere for students and scholars.

🛕
06

Kanyaka Parameswari Temple

Santha Pet

Dedicated to Goddess Kanyaka Parameswari. Highly revered by the local community, drawing large gatherings during festivals.

🛕
07

Jagannath Temple

Santha Pet

Dedicated to Lord Jagannath. Renowned for its vibrant Rath Yatra chariot procession that draws devotees from across the region.

🛕
08

Gumchi Temple

Hill Area

Dedicated to Lord Hanuman. A hilltop temple surrounded by lush greenery, offering both spiritual solace and scenic views.

🛕
09

Ramathirtham

Nellimarla Mandal

An ancient sacred site linked with Lord Rama and Jain heritage. One of the oldest pilgrimage destinations near Vizianagaram.
