Religious Places in Vizianagaram

Ramanarayanam

Korukonda Road, Vizianagaram

Represents the life and values of Lord Rama. Calm and peaceful environment.

Subrahmanyaswamy Temple

Phool Bagh Colony Road

Dedicated to Lord Subrahmanya. Believed to fulfill prayers for health and success.

Pydithalli Temple

Three Lamps Area

Dedicated to Goddess Pydithalli Ammavaru. Important during Dasara.

Dwadasa Jyotirlinga Temple

SVN Nagar

Dedicated to Lord Shiva. All 12 Jyotirlingas at one place.

Gnyana Saraswati Temple

Ring Road, SVN Nagar

Dedicated to Goddess Saraswati. Peaceful devotional atmosphere.

Kanyaka Parameswari Temple

Santha Pet

Dedicated to Goddess Kanyaka Parameswari. Highly revered.

Jagannath Temple

Santha Pet

Dedicated to Lord Jagannath. Special Rath Yatra celebrations.

Gumchi Temple

Hill Area

Dedicated to Lord Hanuman. Hilltop temple with greenery.

Ramathirtham

Nellimarla Mandal

Ancient site linked with Lord Rama and Jain heritage.
