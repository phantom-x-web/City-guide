VIZIANAGARAM INTRODUCTION

Vizianagaram is a historic city in northeastern Andhra Pradesh known for its royal heritage, classical music culture, and iconic landmarks like Vizianagaram Fort. It offers a calm and traditional atmosphere with rich cultural roots.

Vizianagaram Fort

Built in 1713 by the Pusapati kings, this fort reflects the royal history of the city.

Ramnarayanam Temple

A uniquely bow-shaped temple dedicated to Lord Rama with beautiful Ramayana sculptures.

Ramatheertham

An ancient hill pilgrimage site with temples and Buddhist ruins dating to the 3rd century BC.

Tatipudi Reservoir

A reservoir on the Gosthani River supplying water and known for scenic beauty.

Clock Tower

A British-era landmark in the city center representing colonial history.

Bobbili Fort

A 17th-century fort famous for the historic Battle of Bobbili.

Thotapalli Barrage

A major irrigation project supporting farmers in Vizianagaram district.
