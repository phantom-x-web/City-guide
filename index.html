VIZIANAGARAM INTRODUCTION:-

Vizianagaram is a city located in the northeastern part of Andhra Pradesh, India, close to the Bay of Bengal and near the coastal city of Visakhapatnam. Its location makes it easily accessible and well connected to major parts of the state.

The city is known for its rich royal heritage and the historic Vizianagaram Fort, which stands as a symbol of its glorious past. Vizianagaram has also played an important role in the growth of Indian classical music, especially Carnatic music.

Vizianagaram is a historic and cultural city with a calm and traditional atmosphere.
